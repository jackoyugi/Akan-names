AkAN GHANA NOMENCLATURE


Akan names are given to people in Ghana and it inherits a culture of giving names depending on the birth day of the week. Female names starts with letter "A" while males starts with letter "K".

Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born. Luckily for us, there are ways to calculate the day of the week from a specific date like this method a bove!
